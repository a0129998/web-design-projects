Sleep Buddy

Sleep Buddy aims to help people kick the bad habbit of oversleeping, without reducing the amount of sleep you get.
So that you will always be alert when you are awake, and be more productive.
The central idea is just to kick the bad habit, and we'll help you.
Previous Next

The Problem

People prefer to oversleep when they are supposed to get up, most probably from not sleeping early enough (playing computer games, reading books or other activities which caused them to lose track of time the previous night) or being deeply ingrained in bad sleeping habits such as instinctively snoozing the alarm. This may result in them missing important events due to being late.

The Idea

Target users:

People who have problem with their sleep schedule, but wants to make a change and are motivated to take action.

The Solution:

A “Sleep buddy” phone application which provides users with information and the best ways to overcome their sleep problem. It will also create small and easily achievable milestone goals for the user to accomplish and make them more aware of their ability to change for the better when they do so. This will help generate intrinsic motivation for them to change their sleeping habits. By providing step by step guides to users which strive to make small changes to the user’s sleeping habits one at a time, the app hopes to help the user make a permanent alteration to the user’s sleeping habits. These guides will begin with the first step of helping the user in sleeping early and consequently, waking up earlier.
